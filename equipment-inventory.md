## Equipment Request Process

1. Submit equipment request to your supervisor
2. Supervisor approves and forwards to Equipment Manager
3. Equipment is checked out and logged
4. Return equipment and report any damage immediately

## Maintenance Schedule

- **Daily**: Visual inspection of assigned equipment
- **Weekly**: Vehicle maintenance checks
- **Monthly**: Firearms cleaning and inspection
- **Quarterly**: Body armor inspection
- **Annually**: Equipment certification and training

---

## Notes

- All equipment must be properly maintained and secured
- Report any damaged or malfunctioning equipment immediately
- Unauthorized equipment modifications are prohibited
- Lost or stolen equipment must be reported to a supervisor immediately
